The collection of blood is not a random process, but is related to the actual need for blood and drainage, and the success of this process lies in facilitating the arrival of samples to the places where the blood collection is concentrated. And due to the lack of awareness in society today about the importance of donating blood, and the lack of blood stores in hospitals, we created a Drop application. A Drop application that acts as a mediator between the donor and the blood bank. A Drop that makes it easier for the donor to donate while at home via trucks that transport samples from the donorâ€™s location to the blood bank. The application includes the main application page that shows advertisements of the latest initiatives and seminars, personal pages of users and most users who donate blood and a map that includes the locations of all hospitals that receive donations.
